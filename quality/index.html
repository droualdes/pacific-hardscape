Paving Stones

Within 30 Days from Completion: less than 1/4-inch.
After 90 Days from Completion: less than 1/4-inch in depth.

Synthetic Grass

Materials

Our synthetic lawns are environmentally safe and contain no toxic properties.

Installation

Our synthetic grass is natural in appearance, but may include minor wave-like rises and depressions.

After a moderate to heavy rain storm (up to 3-inches per hour), drainage should occur and there should be no standing water with our synthetic grass.

Our synthetic grass is generally weed-free, however, seeds can be carried by the wind and dropped onto the grass. Simply remove these seeds by hand to keep them from growing.

Synthetic Grass is low-maintenance, however, it should be cleaned and groomed periodically depending on frequency of use. High traffic areas of synthetic grass may require occasional professional grooming.
