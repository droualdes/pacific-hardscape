Patios

Designed & Built by Pacific Pavers & Stone

Feel great about being in your backyard and entertain to your hearts content with a patio from Pacific Pavers and Stone. When you deal with us, you get fantastic customer service, superior building practices, and a great looking patio.

Although we highly recommend building your patio with modern interlocking pavers for their many advantages, we can alternatively build your patio with traditional products, such as flagstone, ground tiles*, and concrete. Regardless of materials chosen, your patio will be built to the highest standards.

For pavers, check out our Belgard, Calstone, McNear, and Pavestone paver product lines for thousands of combined styles, colors, and patterns.
