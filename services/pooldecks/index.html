Pool Decks

Designed & Built by Pacific Pavers & Stone

Enjoy the beauty of being poolside with a pool deck from Pacific Pavers and Stone. When you deal with us, you get fantastic customer service, superior building practices, and a great looking pool deck.

Although we highly recommend building your pool deck with modern interlocking pavers for their many advantages, we can alternatively build your pool deck with traditional products, such as flagstone, ground tiles, and concrete. Regardless of materials chosen, your pool deck will be built to the highest standards.

For pavers, check out our Belgard, Calstone, McNear, and Pavestone paver product lines for thousands of combined styles, colors, and patterns.
