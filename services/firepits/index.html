Fire Pits

Designed & Built by Pacific Hardscapes

Snuggle up to a cozy fire in a fire pit from Pacific Hardscapes. We offer both custom-built and economical fire pits options. Our custom-built fire pits are designed and built according to your specifications with either segmental blocks or traditional masonry products, while our prefabricated fire pits come as-is from the manufacturer.

We can design and build fire pits built using segmental block and traditional masonry products. For segmental blocks, check out of our Belgard, Calstone, McNear, and Pavestone product lines for an array of block styles, colors, and patterns.
