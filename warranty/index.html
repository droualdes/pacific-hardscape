Materials & Installation Warranty

Pacific Pavers and Stone stands behind our materials and installation with an incredible warranty as described below.

Interlocking Pavers

We offer a 25-year installation warranty and a lifetime manufacturer's warranty on interlocking pavers. If within this warranty period, the workmanship of the installation fails to conform to our Installation Quality Specifications or there is a defect in the interlocking pavers, we will, at our discretion, choose to repair or replace the interlocking pavers at our own expense.

Segmental Retaining & Freestanding Walls

We offer a 5-year installation warranty and a lifetime manufacturer's warranty on segmental retaining wall blocks. If within this warranty period, the workmanship of the installation fails to conform to Installation Quality Specifications or there is a defect in the segmental retaining wall blocks, we will, at our discretion, choose to repair or replace the segmental retaining wall blocks at our own expense.

Synthetic Grass

We offer a 5-year installation warranty and a 7-10 year manufacturer's warranty on synthetic turf depending upon the type of synthetic grass installed. If within this warranty period, the workmanship of the installation fails to conform to our Installation Quality Specifications or there is a defect in the grass, we will, at our discretion, choose to repair or replace the grass at our own expense.

Condition of Warranty

For this warranty to be effective:

  • Once a problem becomes known, customer must notify us immediately. We will not be responsible for repairing additional damage caused by failure to notify us in a timely manner.
  • This warranty only covers defects in the products or the installation, such as, but not limited to, causes described in our Home Improvement Agreement.
  • If we needs to replace products, color matches are not guaranteed.
  • Changes made to your products or installation by anyone, but Pacific Pavers and Stone, voids your warranty.
  • Normal maintenance, including, but not limited to cleaning, weeding, and turf grooming or changes in lighting caused by changes in landscaping or vegetation growth, is not covered under this warranty.
  • Products manufactured by third parties, except for pavers, retaining wall blocks, and synthetic and grass, are not covered under this warranty.
  • Upon the completion of the work, warranty is void if payment is not met per your Home Improvement Agreement with Pacific Pavers and Stone.
