Pavers Warranty

Interlocking pavers installations come with an Installation Warranty and a Manufacturer's Warranty.

25-Year Installation Warranty

Pacific Hardscapes offers a 25-year warranty on the installation of interlocking pavers. If within this warranty period, the workmanship of the installation fails to conform to our Installation Standards (shown below) or there is a defect in the pavers, we will, at our discretion, choose to repair or replace the pavers at our own expense.

Installation Standards

Pavers installations meet all National Concrete Masonry Association (NCMA) and Interlocking Concrete Pavement Institue (ICPI) specifications, unless otherwise specified and agreed to by the customer.

Grade deviation (sinking/flatness)

  • Within 30 Days from Completion: < 1/2" measured with 10' straight edge. This doesn't apply to grade changes for drainage.
  • After 90 Days from Completion: < 1/2" measured with 10'straight edge. This doesn't apply to grade changes for drainage.

Edge restraint separation

  • Within 30 Days from Completion: 1/16" to 3/16". This may be wider with tumbled products.
  • After 90 Days from Completion: < 1/4". This may be wider with tumbled products.

Width between paver joints

  • Within 30 Days from Completion: 1/16" to 3/16". This may be wider with tumbled products.
  • After 90 Days from Completion: < 1/4". This may be wider with tumbled products.

Sand loss between joints (joint depth)

  • Within 30 Days from Completion: < 1/4" to 1/2". This may be wider with tumbled products.
  • After 90 Days from Completion: < 1/2". This may be wider with tumbled products.

Rutting

  • Within 30 Days from Completion: < 1/4". After 90 Days from Completion: < 1/4" in depth.

Exceptions

  • These standards do not apply to the movement of pavers due to earthquakes, landslides, flooding, or other such acts of god.
