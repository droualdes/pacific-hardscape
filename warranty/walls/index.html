Segmental Retaining Wall Warranty

All segmental retaining wall installations come with an Installation Warranty and a Manufacturer's Warranty.

5-Year Installation Warranty

Pacific Hardscapes offers a 5-year warranty on the installation of segmental retaining walls. If within this warranty period, the workmanship of the installation fails to conform to our Installation Standards (shown below) or there is a defect in the wall blocks, we will, at our discretion, choose to repair or replace the wall blocks at our own expense.

Installation Standards

All segmental retaining walls installations meet all National Concrete Masonry Association (NCMA) and Interlocking Concrete Pavement Institue (ICPI) specifications, unless otherwise specified and agreed to by the customer.

Exceptions

These standards do not apply to the movement of walls or wall blocks due to earthquakes, landslides, flooding, or other such acts of god.

Manufacturer's Warranty

The wall blocks are warrantied by their manufacturer. Please refer to your manufacturer's website if your paver manufacturer is not shown.

Belgard

Belgard basically guarantees their wall blocks for life. View the Belgard website specific warranty details.

Calstone

Calstone basically guarantees their wall blocks for as long as you own your home. View the Calstone website for specific warranty details.

Pavestone
