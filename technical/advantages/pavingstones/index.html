Pacific Pavers and Stone has the experience and wisdom of thousands of installations under their belts. Our installers are professionally trained and certified in proper installation techniques. We only recommend products that pass our high-quality standards.

Strength & Durability

Paving Stones are roughly 4X stronger than concrete. These paving stones will not crack like concrete—even in harsh weather conditions.

Economics

Interlocking Paving Stone installation are affordable and make long-term economic sense. In fact, the lifespan of an interlocking paving stone installation is many times longer than a concrete, asphalt, or flagstone installation.

Choice, Choices, Choices

Limitless design possibilities exist with interlocking paving stones compared to concrete, asphalt, or flagstone. Selections include a variety of styles, colors, shapes, and textures.

Subsurface Repairs

Just remove any interlocking paving stones above a broken pipe or tree root pushing up on the surface and put them back when you are done making repairs. That’s impossible to do with concrete or asphalt. There’s no loss of investment as the paving stones are reused.

Earthquake Performance

In the event of an Earthquake, interlocking paving stones can be reused if displaced, thus minimizing any loss of investment. This makes them the perfect solution for those living in California.
