Installation Quality Specifications

All installations shall be made in accordance with the specifications attached to your Home Improvement Agreement.

Paving Stones

Materials:

All paving stones are Built-Smart approved materials and meets all National Concrete Masonry Association (NCMA) and Interlocking Concrete Pavement Institue (ICPI) specifications, unless otherwise specified and agreed to by customer.

Installation:

Grade deviation (sinking/flatness)

Within 30 Days from Completion: < 1/2" measured with 10' straight edge.*

After 90 Days from Completion: < 1/2" measured with 10'straight edge.*

*Doesn't apply to grade changes for drainage.

Edge restraint separation

Within 30 Days from Completion: < 1/16" to 3/16".*

After 90 Days from Completion: < 1/4".*

*This may be wider with tumbled products.

Width between paver joints

Within 30 Days from Completion: < 1/16" to 3/16".*

After 90 Days from Completion: < 1/4".*

*This may be wider with tumbled products.

Sand loss between joints (joint depth)

Within 30 Days from Completion: < 1/4" to 1/2".*

After 90 Days from Completion: < 1/2"

*This may be wider with tumbled products.

Rutting

Within 30 Days from Completion: <1/4"
After 90 Days from Completion: <1/4" in depth

Segmental Retaining Walls

Materials:

All segmental retaining wall materials meets all National Concrete Masonry Association (NCMA) and Interlocking Concrete Pavement Institue (ICPI) specifications.

Installation:

Segmental retaining wall installations are done according to the National Concrete Masonry Association (NCMA) and Interlocking Concrete Pavement Institue (ICPI) specifications that exist at the time of installation.

Synthetic Grass

Materials:

Our synthetic lawns are environmentally safe and contain no toxic properties.

Installation:

Our synthetic grass is natural in appearance, but may include minor wave-like rises and depressions.

After a moderate to heavy rain storm (up to 3 inches per hour), drainage should occur and there should be no standing water with our synthetic grass.

Our synthetic grass is generally weed-free, however, seeds can be carried by the wind and dropped onto the grass. Simply remove these seeds by hand to keep them from growing.

Synthetic Grass is low-maintenance, however, it should be cleaned and groomed periodically depending on frequency of use. High traffic areas of synthetic grass may require occasional professional grooming.
